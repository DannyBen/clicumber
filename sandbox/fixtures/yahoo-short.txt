Yahoo Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in 
Sunnyvale, California. It is globally known for its Web portal, search engine
Yahoo! Search, and related services, including Yahoo! Directory, Yahoo! Mail,
Yahoo! Wikipedia

Founded: March 2, 1995, Santa Clara, California, United States
Headquarters: Sunnyvale, California, United States
Revenue: 4.61 billion USD (2014)
Chairman: Maynard Webb
Founders: Jerry Yang, David Filo
